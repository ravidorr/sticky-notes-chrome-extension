Context is everything.

Traditional sticky note apps float on top of your screen. Sticky Notes floats on top of your content, respecting the layout and scrolling with the page.

Element Anchoring

Our smart selector engine binds notes to specific DOM elements, so they survive page reloads and layout shifts.

Visibility Intelligence

Notes only appear when the anchored element enters your viewport. No screen clutter.

Team Collaboration

Share notes via email or create team groups. See updates in real-time as your colleagues edit comments.

Responsive Aware

Notes adjust their position intelligently to prevent going off-screen, even when you resize the browser.

Private by Default

Your notes are private to you until you choose to share them. Built on secure Firebase Authentication.

Instant Sync

Notes are saved to the cloud instantly. Switch devices or refresh the page, and your context is right there.

Three clicks to clarity

From confusion to actionable bug report in seconds.

01

Activate Selection

Click the extension icon or use the hotkey to enter 'Target Mode'. The page elements will highlight as you hover.

02

Click to Anchor

Select the specific image, paragraph, or button you want to annotate. The note snaps to the top-right corner.

03

Collaborate

Type your note and mention teammates. They'll see the note exactly where you placed it on their screen.
