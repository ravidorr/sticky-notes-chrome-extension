Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Sticky Notes.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore with encryption in transit and at rest. Your notes are private by default and only shared when you explicitly choose to share them.

Which browsers are supported?

Sticky Notes is available as a Chrome extension. Support for Firefox and Edge is on our roadmap.

Can I use it without an account?

Yes! You can use Sticky Notes locally without signing in. Your notes will be stored in your browser. Sign in with Google to sync across devices and share with teammates.

How does element anchoring work?

We use smart CSS selectors to identify elements. When a page changes, we re-anchor notes to their original elements using multiple fallback strategies to ensure your notes stay in place.

Is there a trial period?

The Starter plan is free forever with up to 10 cloud notes. Team and Pro plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.

Can I export my notes?

Export functionality is coming soon. For now, you can access all your notes through the extension and copy content as needed.
